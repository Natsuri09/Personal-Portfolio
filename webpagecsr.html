About Our College

The College of Science and Technology (CST) is the first institute in the country to offer undergraduate degree programmes in engineering under the Royal University of Bhutan. The college aspires to be a centre of excellence in the field of science and technology-enriched with GNH values by offering quality programmes that are relevant to the need of the job market both within and outside the country.

About Admission

STUDENTS WITHOUT CID ARE NOT ELIGIBLE FOR ADMISSION.

For Architect Programme:

Class XII pass students with a minimum of 50% in Physics, Chemistry, Math, English, and Dzongkha are eligible. An aptitude test will be administered. Students must achieve a minimum of 50% in the test to be eligible for the programme.

Ability Ratings: Math (5), Physics (5), Chemistry (2), English (3), and Aptitude Test.

For Engineering Programmes [Civil, Electrical, ECE, ICE, Engineering Geology, Mechanical, IT, and Water Resource Engineering]:

Class XII pass students with a minimum of 50% in Physics, Chemistry, Math, English, and Dzongkha are eligible. A CTC test will be administered.

Ability Ratings: Math (5), Physics (5), Chemistry (4), English (3), and one other subject (1).
